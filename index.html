Tournaments

Tournaments are held on our server every month. The prizes are as follows: 1st place - $350, 2nd place - Kalash Azymov, 3rd place - $50 . Each team should have five players. First, you must go to 1 qualifying round to enter the winners' bracket. 16 teams meet in the winners' bracket and compete for an exit in 1/8 and so on until the semi-finals in the semi-finals 4 teams and only 2 pass. The last team won.

Availability
