Our project

The CS:GO online server "Brouhny" is the first project created by our team, and we are proud of its development and launch. This server was created to provide players with a unique and exciting gaming experience in the world of Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. We paid special attention to the details and functionality of the "Brouhny" server. We have developed a variety of game modes so that players can enjoy different playstyles, whether it's classic matches, capture the flag, or battle of teams. We have also ensured that the gameplay is balanced so that all players can enjoy a fair and fair game. In addition, we have created a friendly and welcoming atmosphere on the "Brouhny" server, where players can make new friends, chat and interact with each other. We maintain zero tolerance for cheating, cheating and abuse to create a safe and enjoyable gaming environment for everyone. Our team continues to actively develop the "Brouhny" server, introducing new features, improving game balance and listening to feedback from our players. We strive to make the server the best place to play CS:GO and create a community where every player can find their place. We thank all the players who joined our "Brouhny" server and support us in our first project. Without you, our server would not be able to achieve such success. We will continue to work hard to provide you with the best gaming experience and make "Brouhny" one of the best CS:GO servers.
