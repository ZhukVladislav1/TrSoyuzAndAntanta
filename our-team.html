Our team

The Robocode team is a group of talented programmers passionate about the art of robot design and programming. We specialize in participation in competitions and projects related to Robocode. Robocode is a programming platform where members create their own virtual robots and face them off in epic arena battles. It's a fun and exciting way to apply your programming, algorithmic thinking, and strategic planning skills. Your "Robocode" team is committed to excellence in this area. We learn various programming languages ​​such as Java, Python and C++ and apply them to create smart and powerful robots that can win battles. We regularly participate in Robocode competitions where our robots compete against other teams from all over the world. We strive to create innovative strategies, efficient algorithms, and unique development approaches to excel in these competitions. In addition, our team actively exchanges knowledge and experience in the field of Robocode programming. We conduct internal training sessions, discuss new techniques and share tips to constantly improve our skills and reach new heights in this exciting field. We are proud of our "Robocode" team and strive to be leaders in the world of robot programming and development. We are constantly evolving and our passion for Robocode never ends.
