Donats

Advertising benefits: If you donate, we provide the opportunity to advertise your personal projects, streams or communities on our server. This will help you get more visibility for your content and bring in new users or viewers. We always strive to provide our players with the best gaming experience, and your support through our donation system allows us to make the "Brouhny" server even better. Thank you for choosing our offers and we hope you enjoy the benefits they provide.

VIP status

We offer players to become VIP users by acquiring a special status for a certain amount. As a VIP player, you will have access to exclusive features such as bonus experience, additional items, or privileged access to teams. This will allow you to enjoy a more unique and varied gaming experience.

In-Game Currency

We have our own in-game currency that players can purchase by donating. This in-game currency gives you the ability to purchase upgrades, cosmetic items, new weapons, and other in-game benefits. As a donator, you will be able to access exclusive items that will help you stand out on the server.

Cosmetic Items

We offer unique cosmetic items that players can receive in exchange for donations. These can be unique skins, emblems or weapon models that will be visually appealing and help you stand out from other players on the server.
